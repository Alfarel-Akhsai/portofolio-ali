About Me

Hi, I'm M. Alfarel—but you can call me AL. Currently, I'm navigating a unique academic journey as a double degree student, studying Informatics Engineering at Institut Teknologi Sumatera (ITERA) and Business at BINUS University.

My path into technology wasn't sparked by an instant love or a natural talent for coding. Instead, I deliberately chose this field with a strong determination to learn, adapt, and master programming from the ground up. Today, I'm actively building my skills in C++, Python, and web development.

Beyond writing lines of code, I have a deep passion for customizing Linux (Ubuntu) systems and possess a strong practical talent for troubleshooting and repairing damaged computers and laptops. Whether I'm exploring biomimicry in engineering or participating as a Google Student Ambassador candidate and in the Novo Club, I am always eager to challenge myself and gain a holistic perspective on how technology operates from the inside out.
